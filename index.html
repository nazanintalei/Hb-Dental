The Real Reasons People Put It Off

Whatever's Been Keeping You Away, We've Heard It.

Most of our new patients haven't seen a dentist in years. Here's how we handle the four most common reasons — so you can stop putting it off.

The worry
"I'm going to get lectured about flossing."
What actually happens
Zero guilt trips. Dr. Christensen meets you where you are — whether it's been 6 months or 6 years. We focus on what to do next, not what you didn't do.
The worry
"I'm going to be upsold on treatments I don't need."
What actually happens
You get a written treatment plan with priorities clearly labeled — needed now, watch, and optional. If you can wait, we'll tell you. That's why families stay for decades.
The worry
"I can't afford it — and I don't have great insurance."
What actually happens
0% financing through CareCredit, in-house payment plans, and transparent pricing before anything starts. Most PPOs accepted. No insurance? Ask about our $99 new patient special.
The worry
"I'm genuinely scared. Last dentist made it worse."
What actually happens
Tell us at check-in. Numbing before the needle, breaks whenever you need them, and a chairside manner built over 27 years of anxious patients becoming regulars.
Still not sure? Just ask.
Text us any question — no appointment needed. Real response, usually within an hour during business hours.
