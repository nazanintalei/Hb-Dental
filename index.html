The Real Reasons People Put It Off

Whatever's Been Keeping You Away, We've Heard It.

Most of our new patients haven't seen a dentist in years. Here's how we handle the four most common reasons — so you can stop putting it off.

The worry
"I'm going to get lectured about flossing."
What actually happens
Zero guilt trips. Dr. Christensen meets you where you are — whether it's been 6 months or 6 years. We focus on what to do next, not what you didn't do.
The worry
"I'm going to be upsold on treatments I don't need."
What actually happens
You get a written treatment plan with priorities clearly labeled — needed now, watch, and optional. If you can wait, we'll tell you. That's why families stay for decades.
The worry
"I can't afford it — and I don't have great insurance."
What actually happens
0% financing through CareCredit, in-house payment plans, and transparent pricing before anything starts. Most PPOs accepted. No insurance? Ask about our $99 new patient special.
The worry
"I'm genuinely scared. Last dentist made it worse."
What actually happens
Tell us at check-in. Numbing before the needle, breaks whenever you need them, and a chairside manner built over 27 years of anxious patients becoming regulars.
Still not sure? Just ask.

Frequently Asked

Questions We Hear Most

Don't see your question? Ask us directly — we'll respond within an hour during business hours.

What insurance do you accept?
We accept most major PPO insurance plans, including Delta Dental, Aetna, Cigna, MetLife, Guardian, and United Healthcare. We also offer 0% in-house financing and CareCredit for patients without insurance. Call us to verify your specific plan — we'll do it in 60 seconds.
How much does a dental exam and cleaning cost?
For new patients, we offer a special $99 package that includes a comprehensive exam, digital X-rays, and professional cleaning — a $379 value. With most PPO plans, preventive care is covered 100%, meaning $0 out of pocket.
Do you see same-day dental emergencies?
Yes. We hold same-day slots every day for emergencies — cracked teeth, severe pain, lost crowns, knocked-out teeth. Call (714) 842-5593 and we'll get you seen as quickly as possible.
Is Dr. Christensen accepting new patients?
Yes — we warmly welcome new patients of all ages, from toddlers to seniors. Book online or call us directly. Your first visit typically takes 60–75 minutes and includes a full exam, X-rays, cleaning, and time with Dr. Christensen to discuss your goals.
How much do dental implants cost in Huntington Beach?
A single dental implant starts at $4,000, which includes the implant, abutment, and crown. Full-arch All-on-4 treatment starts at $18,000 per arch. We offer 0% financing up to 24 months through CareCredit, and every treatment plan comes with transparent pricing in writing — no surprises.
Does Invisalign actually work for adults?
Yes — and the majority of our Invisalign patients are adults. Average treatment is 12–18 months. Dr. Christensen has completed hundreds of adult Invisalign cases. Unlike mail-order aligner services, you're monitored in-person every 6–8 weeks, which is why the results are predictable.
What is Pinhole gum surgery and am I a candidate?
Pinhole is a minimally invasive alternative to traditional gum grafting — no scalpels, no stitches, no donor tissue from the palate. Dr. Christensen is one of very few certified Pinhole providers in Huntington Beach. Results are immediate. Schedule a free consultation to find out if you're a candidate.
I have severe dental anxiety. Can you help?
Absolutely. Tell us when you book so we can prepare. We offer thorough pre-numbing, break-anytime policies, noise-cancelling headphones, and nitrous oxide ("laughing gas") when helpful. Many of our longest-term patients first came in terrified — they stay because they feel safe here.
Where are you located and is parking available?
We're at 6968 Warner Ave, Huntington Beach, CA 92647 — at the corner of Warner and Goldenwest, with free on-site parking. Easy access from the 405 (Goldenwest exit) and from Costa Mesa, Fountain Valley, Westminster, and surrounding areas.
What are your hours?
Monday–Friday, 8 AM to 5 PM. Saturdays available by appointment for established patients. Closed Sundays. Early-morning and lunch-hour appointments available — just ask.
